# WarpX Handoff Checklist

- [ ] Record authorized execution host, module/container, and WarpX version.
- [ ] Confirm grid spacing, timestep, and Courant constraint.
- [ ] Confirm species charge/mass, particles per cell, and boundary conditions.
- [ ] Capture stdout/stderr and openPMD metadata paths.
- [ ] Parse field energy, Courant value, particle count, and warning events.
- [ ] Attach dashboard/result/failure Note only after execution evidence exists.
