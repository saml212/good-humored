## Outline sanity checks (run before drafting)

- [ ] Page budgets sum to the venue limit (excluding references and appendix).
- [ ] Every claim id in the brief's evidence map appears in exactly one section.
- [ ] Every figure in the brief is placed in a section.
- [ ] Related work distinguishes this paper from each nearest neighbor by name.
- [ ] No section carries a claim whose evidence row is missing or pending.
